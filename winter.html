DESTINATIONS

Lapland

Lapland is a winter wonderland that offers a unique and unforgettable experience. It's known for its picturesque landscapes, Northern Lights, reindeer sleigh rides, and a chance to stay in an igloo or glass-roofed cabin. There are also many winter sports activities such as skiing, snowmobiling, and ice fishing. Lapland is a great place to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and enjoy the serenity of nature.

Banff

Banff is a winter paradise that offers stunning mountain scenery and plenty of winter sports activities. It's known for its world-renowned ski resorts, ice skating, snowshoeing, and ice walks. Banff is also home to the famous Banff Hot Springs, which are open year-round and offer a perfect way to relax and rejuvenate after a day of winter activities. The town of Banff also has a charming downtown area with great shopping and dining options.

EVENTS

Christmas Markets: During the winter season, several Christmas markets are held in Lapland, including in Rovaniemi and Levi. These markets offer traditional Finnish handicrafts, local food and drinks, and plenty of festive cheer.

Reindeer Races: This is a traditional Lapland winter event that takes place in several locations throughout the region. It involves watching reindeer and their riders compete in a race, with the first team to cross the finish line being declared the winner.

Ice Magic Festival: Held every January in Lake Louise, the Ice Magic Festival is a celebration of ice sculpture. World-renowned ice carvers from around the globe come to create stunning ice sculptures that are displayed in a winter wonderland setting.
